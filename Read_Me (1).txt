
                                                                                                  (Tic Tac Toe Game)

##general information

 A tic tac toe game (x,o) with computer or with a friend online 



##steps to play this game 

*You should firstly sign up to the game by user name and password .

*Then login to the game by  your user name and password.

*You will be entred to the home page of the game enable you to choose the mode you want to play.

*In the single mode there are two levels (easy and hard) to play with computer.

*In the multi players mode you should invite a friend on the online list and start play with him .
